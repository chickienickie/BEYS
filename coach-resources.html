PA Required Clearances

Thank you for volunteering to coach for Bald Eagle Youth Soccer. As a coach for a PA West affiliate, there are several mandatory requirements. Please complete all items below and upload the required documents.

Introduction to Grassroots Course

Via the USSF Digital Coaching Center.
Sign up for access to training. Under Courses, click Introduction. Take a screenshot of the completed course and email it.

SafeSport – Abuse Awareness

PA West Soccer requires all coaches/volunteers/referees/employees to complete abuse awareness training.

SafeSport Training

Free for volunteers, valid 5 years. Complete and upload the report.

PA State Police Report

Child Abuse History Report

PA Child Abuse History Clearance requests may be made online.

Child Abuse History Report

FBI Criminal Background Check

Only required if you haven’t been a PA resident for 10+ years.

Use Identogo volunteer service code: 1KG6ZJ. Approx. cost: $21.35.

FBI Clearance (Identogo)

If you have been a PA resident for 10+ years, fill out the Volunteer Qualification Affidavit (notarized).

Note: Clearances are valid for 5 years and must be valid for the full season.
