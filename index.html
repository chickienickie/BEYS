SOCCER CLEAT EXCHANGE

Need cleats? We’ve got you covered! BEYS offers a selection of gently used cleats (worn for one season or less) available free of charge for our players. Be sure to check out what’s available before you buy!

Have cleats your child has outgrown? We gladly accept donations to help other players.

We also have a limited supply of shin guards and goalkeeper gloves—just let us know if you need any!

Play, share, and support each other! 🦅⚽

NEW THIS SEASON
FOR U11 & U13 TEAMS!

We’re excited to introduce something extra fun this year!

Our U11 and U13 teams will be taking part in Friendly Thursday Games in partnership with the Centre Soccer Association. These games are all about adding more excitement, building confidence, and giving players extra opportunities to grow through real game experience.

Here’s what to expect:

⚽ One friendly game added to each team’s schedule this season

📅 Games will take place during one of their regular Thursday practices

🤝 A great mix of fun, skill-building, and friendly competition
