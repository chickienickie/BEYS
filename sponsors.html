Our Sponsors

Become a Sponsor

Bald Eagle Youth Soccer is looking for Sponsors! Your generosity helps us run fall and spring soccer seasons—we need YOU!

We are a volunteer-run organization. Donations and sponsorships help us purchase supplies to maintain our home soccer fields, provide jerseys and essential gear, and keep registration costs affordable for local families.
